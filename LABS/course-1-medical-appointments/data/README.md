# Dataset: Medical Appointment No Shows

**Source:** Kaggle - Medical Appointment No Shows  
**URL:** https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/joniarroba/noshowappointments  
**Date Downloaded:** 2026-02-14  
**Size:** ~3 MB (110,527 rows × 14 columns)

## Description
This dataset collects information from 100k medical appointments in Brazil 
and is focused on the question of whether or not patients show up for their 
appointment.

## Clinical Context
As a physician with 15+ years in oncology and palliative care, appointment 
adherence is critical for treatment outcomes. This dataset allows me to 
apply data science to predict and prevent no-shows.

## Columns
- PatientId: Unique patient identifier
- AppointmentID: Unique appointment identifier  
- Gender: Male/Female
- ScheduledDay: When appointment was booked
- AppointmentDay: Actual appointment date
- Age: Patient age
- Neighbourhood: Hospital location
- Scholarship: Welfare recipient (Bolsa Família)
- Hipertension: Hypertension diagnosis
- Diabetes: Diabetes diagnosis
- Alcoholism: Alcoholism diagnosis
- Handcap: Disability status
- SMS_received: Reminder messages sent
- No-show: Target variable (Yes/No)
